# Atlas Architecture

## Mission

Atlas transforms market evidence into actionable business intelligence.

Atlas is not an SEO platform.

Atlas is not an AI ranking tracker.

Atlas is an Intelligence Platform that helps organizations understand how AI, public information, competitors, and market trends influence purchasing decisions.

---

# Core Principle

Everything inside Atlas revolves around one object:

## Evidence

Evidence may come from:

- AI responses
- News
- Reviews
- Retail websites
- Product manuals
- Videos
- Forums
- Internal documentation
- Industry events

Evidence is never modified.

Evidence is only collected.

Everything else is derived from evidence.

- Buying Journeys
- Industry Taxonomy
