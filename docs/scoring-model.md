# Atlas Scoring Model v0.1

## Atlas Visibility Score

- Mention Rate: 25%
- Average Rank: 20%
- Sentiment: 15%
- Category Coverage: 15%
- Feature Association: 10%
- Model Consistency: 10%
- Momentum: 5%

## Opportunity Score

- Business Relevance: 30%
- Firman Visibility Gap: 25%
- Competitor Strength: 20%
- Content Gap: 15%
- Trend Momentum: 10%

## Interpretation

The score should prioritize business decisions, not vanity metrics. A topic with low Firman visibility but high business relevance should outrank a topic where visibility is already strong.
